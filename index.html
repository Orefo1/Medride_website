About Medride

Who we are

MedRide by Miracle is a compassionate medical transportation service founded by Eliana Miracle Melekwe, a University of Toronto student inspired by her mother, a hardworking nurse. We provide safe, reliable, and wheelchair-accessible rides for nurses, patients, and caregivers across Ontario. Our mission is to make every journey easier, helping healthcare heroes and those they care for reach their destinations with comfort, dignity, and peace of mind. At MedRide, we’re more than a ride, we’re care in motion.
