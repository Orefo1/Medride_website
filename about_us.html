    Our Guiding Values

    Our Focus and Aspirations.

    We aspire to become the leading medical ride service recognized for excellence, comfort, and care, ensuring that every journey supports better health and peace of mind.

    Our Mission

    To bridge the gap between care and comfort by offering safe, dependable, and wheelchair-accessible transportation for nurses, caregivers, and patients. We aim to ease the stress of long shifts, hospital visits, and daily commutes, ensuring every ride is guided by compassion, reliability, and trust.

    Our Vision

    We thrive to become Canada’s most trusted medical transportation partner, empowering healthcare professionals and patients through accessible, human-centered mobility solutions. We envision a future where no one in healthcare faces exhaustion, isolation, or transportation barriers — because every caregiver and patient deserves comfort on the road to care.

    Founder's Message

    From Eliana Miracle Melekwe.

    Founder & CEO of MedRide by Miracle

    Growing up, I watched my mother, Queen Idris, a single mom and a nurse give everything she had to care for others. After working 16-hour shifts, there were nights she’d call me, too exhausted to drive herself home. Sometimes I’d stay up waiting, knowing I’d have to pick her up or drop her off again before sunrise. Other times, I’d drive her and other nurses to their workplaces just so they could get there safely and rest on the way.

    Those long nights stayed with me. I saw not just the physical exhaustion, but the deep emotional weight carried by so many healthcare workers like my mom, people who care for others endlessly, yet often have no one to care for them in return.

    That’s where the idea for MedRide by Miracle was born.

    I wanted to build something that would make life easier for nurses, caregivers, and patients. something that would lighten the load even just a little. MedRide isn’t just a transportation service. It’s a support system on wheels. Our mission is simple: to ensure that healthcare professionals and patients can get to where they need to be safely, comfortably, and with dignity.

    Today, MedRide by Miracle operates in Sudbury, Ontario, with a growing fleet of wheelchair-accessible medical vans that transport both patients and nurses to and from hospitals, clinics, and long-term care facilities. By the end of this month, we’ll officially launch with two accessible vans, one designed to seat six passengers and six wheelchairs, and another with even greater capacity.

    This company is more than a business to me, it’s a reflection of my mother’s strength, sacrifice, and heart, and a promise to continue her legacy of care in a new way.

    To every nurse, every patient, and every caregiver who gives their all, MedRide by Miracle was built for you.
